This Shiny app presents the results from a COVID-19 Booster Vaccine Campaign study

The presented ShinyApp was developed supported by the Shiny package. This helps us assess the research-readiness of a set of cohorts we have defined. 
This assessment, with each corresponding modifications, i.e., as it is exhibited, includes:

- **Summary**  
  Collects the imported Results, providing not only the type of summary, but also its internal or structure. 

- **OmopSketch**  
  Helps to better understand the database in which the cohorts were created. Includes size, time coverage, and number of individuals.
- **Objective 1: Cohort Characterisation**  
  Provides information about cohort inclusion, exclusion, overlap, and timing.

- **Objective 2: Coverage**  
  Estimates coverage of study cohorts.
